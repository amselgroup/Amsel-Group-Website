About Us

Amsel Engineering is a modern revivalist brand devoted to bringing the golden era of motoring back to life through precision engineering, authentic restoration, and creative craftsmanship. Founded on the belief that automobiles are not just machines but living testaments to human ingenuity and culture. Guided by a deep appreciation for the artistry of vintage engineering, the company specializes in breathing new life into classic cars and motorcycles, restoring them to their former glory while respectfully refining them with today’s performance and reliability standards. It’s a return to purity to the raw, soulful days when every curve, gear, and roar meant something. The founder’s personal connection to this vision runs deep, reflected in his own ride, a beautifully maintained 1984 Ind-Suzuki AX-100, which stands as a symbol of his lifelong love for retro machines.

- Alfin Backer (Founder & Director)

That same affection for vintage simplicity and mechanical honesty drives Amsel Engineering’s culture, where innovation meets nostalgia, and every vehicle tells a story that endures beyond generations. But the company goes beyond engineering; it extends the lifestyle of classic motoring to enthusiasts everywhere through its exclusive merchandise line. This passion-driven collection is designed for fellow automotive nerds, purists, and dreamers who share the love of old-school machines and engineering marvels. From retro-inspired apparel to collectible designs, Amsel’s merchandise bridges the gap between the road and everyday life, giving enthusiasts a tangible way to express their identity and connect with a community bound by heritage and creativity. What makes Amsel Engineering stand out is its blend of old-world craftsmanship and present-day understanding of performance, aesthetics, and authenticity. Every handcrafted detail, from a meticulously tuned carburetor to a polished chrome emblem, embodies dedication and finesseAmsel's merchandise connects the road to daily life, allowing enthusiasts to express their identity and community bond. Amsel Engineering stands out by blending old-world craftsmanship with modern performance, aesthetics, and authenticity. Every detail, from a tuned carburetor to a polished chrome emblem, showcases dedication and finesse.

The team thrives on challenges others consider impractical, sourcing rare parts, decoding obsolete schematics. because for Amsel, difficulty is part of the art. Through these efforts, the company has begun to shape not just a business but a movement that honors the enduring charm of vintage automobiles while fostering innovation rooted in respect for tradition. Amsel Engineering envisions a future where sustainability and heritage co-exist, where restoration is not just a service but a statement. Preserving mechanical history for future generations. By combining classic aesthetics with thoughtfully upgraded technology, Amsel aims to deliver experiences that remind people why they fell in love with driving in the first place. Whether it’s reviving a rusted relic into a masterpiece or creating designs that celebrate iconic machines, Amsel Engineering is dedicated to craftsmanship, authenticity, and community, building not only fine automobiles but a culture that keeps the spirit of motoring alive.

What makes you an automotive enthusiast?

Deep Knowledge

Automotive enthusiasts possess extensive expertise on car models, engine specs, history, and innovations, often engaging in detailed discussions.

Hands-On Involvement

They enjoy working directly on vehicles, from maintenance and repairs to custom modifications, taking pride in their mechanical skills.

Design Appreciation

A keen eye for automotive aesthetics, including sleek lines, curves, and craftsmanship, turns cars into appreciated works of art for them.

Thrill of Performance

The excitement of acceleration, handling, and driving dynamics, whether on roads or tracks fuels their love for high performance experiences.

Strong Community Ties

They thrive in car meets, clubs, forums, and events, building bonds with others who share the passion and exchange knowledge.

Our Motive

Our motive is simple, to create precision driven solutions that embody clarity and purpose in every detail. We believe true innovation comes from refinement, not excess, and that progress should make life simpler, not more complicated. “Keep it simple, complexity isn’t the point” is more than a design philosophy; it’s the way we approach engineering, communication, and problem-solving. By removing clutter and focusing on what truly matters, we build products and systems that work seamlessly in real-world conditions. Our mission is to merge elegant design with practical execution, ensuring that every creation from Amsel Engineering stands as a testament to thoughtful simplicity and enduring excellence.
